Česta pitanja

Frequently asked questions

Odgovori na ono što parovi najčešće pitaju pre nego što rezervišu termin.

Answers to what couples most often ask before booking.

Koliko traje snimanje venčanja?

Standardni paket pokriva ceo dan — od priprema mlade i mladoženje (8-10h ujutru) do recepcije i prvog plesa (najčešće do 24h). Imamo i kraće pakete (samo ceremonija, samo proslava) i produžene (multi-day venčanja, after party).

Kada dobijam finalni video?

Highlights film (3-5 min) isporučujemo u roku od 4-6 nedelja. Full ceremony video i recepcija (ako su deo paketa) u roku od 1-2 nedelje. Same-day teaser klip (1-2 min za društvene mreže) može biti spreman za 24-48h.

Da li radite i foto i video?

Da, nudimo full foto + video pakete sa jednim timom — ekonomičnije nego angažovati dva odvojena studija. Imamo i samo-video pakete za parove koji već imaju fotografa.

Koliko unapred treba rezervisati termin?

Za sezonsko venčanje (maj-oktobar) preporučujemo rezervaciju 6-12 meseci unapred. Van sezone (novembar-april) najčešće možemo prihvatiti termin i sa 2-3 meseca najave. Termin se rezerviše uplatom 30% kapare.

Da li snimate venčanja i van Beograda?

Da, snimamo širom Srbije — Novi Sad, Niš, Subotica, Kragujevac i destinacije. Za lokacije van Beograda dodaje se trošak puta i smeštaja (ako je multi-day). Snimamo i destination venčanja u inostranstvu.

Da li koristite dron za snimanje?

Da, koristimo DJI Mini 5 Pro za cinematic aerials. Dron je dostupan u srednjem i premium paketu. Letenje je u skladu sa SCAA regulativom — uz dozvolu organizatora i lokacije.

How long does wedding filming take?

The standard package covers the whole day — from bride and groom prep (8-10am) to reception and first dance (typically until midnight). We also offer shorter packages (ceremony only, party only) and extended ones (multi-day weddings, after-party).

When do I get the final video?

The highlights film (3-5 min) is delivered within 4-6 weeks. Full ceremony video and reception (if part of the package) within 1-2 weeks. Same-day teaser (1-2 min for social media) can be ready within 24-48h.

Do you do both photo and video?

Yes, we offer full photo + video packages with one team — more economical than hiring two separate studios. We also have video-only packages for couples who already have a photographer.

How far in advance should I book?

For peak season (May-October), we recommend booking 6-12 months in advance. Off-season (November-April), we can usually accept with 2-3 months' notice. Booking is secured with a 30% deposit.

Do you film weddings outside Belgrade?

Yes, we film all over Serbia — Novi Sad, Niš, Subotica, Kragujevac, and destinations. For locations outside Belgrade, travel and lodging costs apply (if multi-day). We also film destination weddings abroad.

Do you use a drone?

Yes, we use DJI Mini 5 Pro for cinematic aerials. The drone is available in the mid and premium packages. Flying is compliant with SCAA regulations — with the organizer's and venue's permission.
