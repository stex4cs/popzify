About the Author

Ivan Popović je stručnjak za audio-video produkciju i digitalni marketing sa više od 8 godina iskustva u industriji. Diplomirao je na Fakultetu dramskih umetnosti, smer Produkcija. Njegova strast za vizuelnim pričanjem priča i kreativnim marketinškim rešenjima dovela ga je do saradnje sa brojnim brendovima i agencijama.

Ivan Popović is an expert in audio-video production and digital marketing with over 8 years of industry experience. He graduated from the Faculty of Dramatic Arts, Production Department. His passion for visual storytelling and creative marketing solutions has led him to collaborate with numerous brands and agencies.

Kroz ovaj blog, Ivan deli svoje znanje, iskustvo i najnovije trendove iz sveta multimedijalne produkcije i digitalnog marketinga.

Through this blog, Ivan shares his knowledge, experience, and the latest trends from the world of multimedia production and digital marketing.
